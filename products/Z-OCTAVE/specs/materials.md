# Materials (Z-OCTAVE)

Purpose: document approved materials and constraints.

- Outer shell: fire-retardant, waterproof, breathable.

- Inner liner: hypoallergenic, skin-safe, low-friction.

- Conductive yarns: silver/graphene blends, wash-safe.

- Harvest layers: piezo/tribo fibers, passive only.

Non-negotiable:

- No batteries.

- No medical claims.

- Fail-open construction.
