# Z-NUMEN — Safety and Perception Policy

## Non-goals (Phase 1 and default posture)

Z-NUMEN **must not**:

- Act as a medical, psychiatric, or therapeutic system
- Diagnose, treat, or target individuals psychologically
- Scrape social data or build user profiles
- Claim **proof** of hidden control, secret societies, or the supernatural
- State **future prediction certainty**
- Declare danger from numbers or patterns alone
- Deploy autonomously, call external providers, or execute cross-project bridges from this validator

## Wording discipline

Prefer **forecast / suggest / route** over “predict the future module” style certainty. Signal language describes **salience in an exercise**, not verdicts about reality.

## Educational framing

Approved phrases include calm-return language such as:

> This is a perception pattern, not proof of hidden control.

Operators must avoid turning pattern literacy into fear messaging. See `data/z_numen_signal_bands.json` → `safe_output_rules`.

## Relationship to Z-Logical Brains

Z-Logical Brains owns the **learning pathway**; Z-NUMEN adds **geometry and repetition literacy**. No patient data flows between them in Phase 1.

## Signals (validator)

| Signal | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **GREEN** | Registries complete; advisory, read-only stance |
| **YELLOW** | Minor doc hygiene; review |
| **BLUE** | AMK decision needed (e.g. profiling/deploy cues in fixtures) |
| **RED** | Forbidden claims surfaced as allowed, or structural safety break |

## Future gates

3D meshes, live canvas tutors, or provider-linked visualization remain **explicitly future-gated** in the symbol registry. No silent promotion to runtime.
