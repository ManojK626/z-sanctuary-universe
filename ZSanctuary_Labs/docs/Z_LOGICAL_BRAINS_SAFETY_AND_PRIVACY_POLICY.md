# Z-Logical Brains Safety and Privacy Policy

## Non-clinical boundary

Z-Logical Brains is an educational and reflection support concept. It must never be framed as diagnosis, therapy, treatment, or medical intervention.

## Child safety policy

- No child data collection by default.
- No account creation in Phase 1.
- No cloud sync in Phase 1.
- No hidden telemetry.
- Parent/guardian mode is future-gated and requires explicit safeguards.

## Privacy rules

- Local read-only validation only in Phase 1.
- No personal identifiers required to run validator.
- No voice, camera, GPS, or background capture.
- No provider calls.

## Consent rules

- Any future personal data flow requires clear and explicit consent.
- Any future child-facing flow requires guardian governance and age-appropriate controls.
- Consent withdrawal and deletion pathways are mandatory before any live storage.

## Age-mode rules

- Stage labels are educational scaffolding only.
- No deterministic outcomes from age stages.
- No "senility treatment" wording or equivalent medical framing.

## Forbidden data flows

- Medical diagnosis outputs
- Therapy claims
- Brain treatment claims
- Prediction certainty claims
- Cloud profile sync
- Live provider transmission of child data

## Allowed Phase 1 actions

- Registry definition
- Safety policy definition
- Read-only validator and report output
- Dashboard indicator signaling

## Future gates

Future capabilities (vault runtime, personal AI memory, teacher/parent dashboards, live providers, voice input, multiplayer) remain blocked until explicit governance approval and security design evidence.

## 14 DRP alignment

Phase 1 respects non-harm, transparency, consent, autonomy, and human dignity by design through strict non-clinical framing and disabled sensitive lanes.

## Rollback rules

- Remove Phase 1 files if validator reports RED and cannot be remediated.
- Reset indicator signal to UNKNOWN if reports are missing.
- Keep no personal runtime state in this phase.
