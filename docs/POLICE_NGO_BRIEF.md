# Z-Sanctuary Brief for Police and NGO Partners

This document explains how Z-Sanctuary supports safe, transparent handling of incidents.
It is not an enforcement system and does not replace human judgment.

---

## What Z-Sanctuary Is

- A local-first governance and audit system.
- A structured de-escalation and documentation framework.
- A tool for clear, explainable decision trails.

---

## What Z-Sanctuary Is Not

- Not a surveillance platform.
- Not a predictive policing tool.
- Not an automated enforcement system.

---

## How It Helps

- Improves clarity of incident records.
- Encourages de-escalation and calm response.
- Ensures consent gates and human oversight.
- Reduces ambiguity with transparent logs.

---

## Safeguards

- Dual-pass governance: SKK + RKPK.
- SEPC gate before any action.
- Human override at all times.
- No action without explicit consent.

---

## Adoption Model

- Works offline or on local networks.
- No centralized data extraction.
- Auditable and explainable by design.
