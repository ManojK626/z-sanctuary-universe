# Pricing and benchmarks — evidence doctrine

**Role:** Source doctrine for **Z-SUSBV** (Sanctuary Universe Self Benchmarks Validation). This file does **not** set prices; it anchors how prices may be justified later.

## Core law (no invented numbers)

- **No benchmark without source** (document, receipt, signed comp, or chartered research — never guess).
- **No price without cost floor** (hosting, support, moderation, legal, compliance assumptions documented).
- **No public price claim** without human pricing review and entitlement alignment.

## Hub posture

- **AMK / human** approves any SKU, billing change, or public copy that implies payment.
- **Z-SUSBV** may **advise** only; it does not rewrite catalog copy or entitlements.
