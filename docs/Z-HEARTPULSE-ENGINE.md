# Z-HeartPulse Engine v1.0 (PREPARE ONLY)

**Status:** PREPARE ONLY
**Domain:** Emotional storytelling and self-reflection
**Canonical technical id:** `z-heartpulse-engine`

## Purpose

`Z-HeartPulse Engine` is a **reflection engine** for AMK emotional growth, story craft, and memory meaning-making.

It is **not** a social scoring engine and **not** a control surface for other people.

Core rule:

> It reflects the heart. It does not control another heart.

---

## Ethical boundaries (non-negotiable)

- No scoring of real people for loyalty/love/intent.
- No prediction that a person "loves you" or "will return."
- No surveillance, tracking, or coercive nudging.
- No publishing private relationship claims as facts.
- Use fictionalized names/initials unless explicit consent exists.
- Frame sensitive history as inner memory and narrative context.

---

## Stage model (love arc)

- `LA` - Awareness / first spark
- `LS` - Sight / visual magnet
- `LF` - Feeling / inner bloom
- `LH` - Heartbeat / body response
- `LE` - Emotional recognition
- `LJ` - Jealousy / attachment hazard (risk)
- `LT` - True love / non-possessive care

Direction:

```text
from attachment and possession
toward presence, care, safety, and freedom
```

---

## Human and animal lens

The engine supports a dual lens for storytelling:

- Animal anchors: presence, loyalty, trust, non-verbal healing
- Human complexity: memory, desire, fear, hope, loss, choice

This lens is for cinema and reflection arcs, not diagnostic claims.

---

## Formula (v1 guidance)

```text
Z-HeartPulse =
(LA + LS + LF + LH + LE)
- LJ_Risk
+ LT_Purity
+ Compassion_Filter
+ Consent_Boundary
+ Memory_Context
```

Interpretation:

- Attraction and feeling are acknowledged.
- Jealousy is handled as risk, not destiny.
- "True" score grows with care and freedom.
- Consent and dignity outrank all emotional intensity.
