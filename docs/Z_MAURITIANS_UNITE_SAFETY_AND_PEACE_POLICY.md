# Z-Mauritians Unite — Safety & Peace Policy (Z-MU-TRUTH-1)

## Scope

This policy governs **all** Z-MU-TRUTH-1 hub artifacts: docs, JSON registries, validators, and future read-only dashboard copy. It does **not** authorise runtime political tooling inside the Sanctuary repo.

## Allowed

- Evidence-first claim rows with honest `source_status` values.
- Peaceful, non-blaming public wording drafts pending verification.
- References to **lawful** complaint concepts (national routes, treaty procedures) as **informational** summaries with primary links added by humans.
- Policy **simulations** clearly labelled as models, not predictions or guarantees.
- Dignity-centred framing: workers, families, and communities without stereotyping groups.

## Z-MU-ADVISOR-1 (framework)

Advisor templates MUST refuse impersonation, legal advice as counsel, harassment, illegal leaks, and protest coordination — see [Z_MU_ADVISOR_SAFETY_AND_AUDIT_POLICY.md](Z_MU_ADVISOR_SAFETY_AND_AUDIT_POLICY.md). Phase 1 has **no** live model and **no** user logging.

## Forbidden (automatic RED posture if encoded as export advice)

- Violence, incitement, intimidation, or threats.
- Harassment, pile-ons, doxxing, stalking, or targeted humiliation.
- Instruction to **illegally** obtain, hold, or distribute classified or stolen materials.
- Economic **sabotage** framing (e.g. “crash the economy” as a tactic).
- Hidden surveillance, wiretap, or nonconsensual recording workflows.
- Ethnic or religious **blame** narratives; collective guilt.
- Serious accusations against **named individuals** framed as **settled fact** without verified public evidence (court findings, sworn inquiry, or equally strong documentation — with AMK/ethics gate).
- Payments, donations, ticketing, or provider-backed messaging automation **from this phase**.
- Deployment, live APIs, scrapers, or autonomous agents.

## Source discipline

Use the rank table in `data/z_mu_source_policy.json`. **Rank E** material is never published as fact—only as a prompt to verify.

## Escalation response

If a contributor uploads inflammatory source material, **do not merge** public-facing rows until the peace filter and verification fields are satisfied. Use `npm run z:mu:truth` as a local gate before doc/JSON PRs that touch this domain.
