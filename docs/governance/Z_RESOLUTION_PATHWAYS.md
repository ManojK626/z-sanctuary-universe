# Z-Resolution Pathways (SEPC-governed)

## Stage 1 - Observe

- collect facts
- avoid assumptions
- detect distress vs malicious intent

## Stage 2 - RKPK Gentle Path (Early)

Questions:

- Can reassurance resolve this?
- Is the person overwhelmed?
- Would slowing down reduce harm?

If resolved, close with kindness and clarity.

## Stage 3 - SKK Structured Path (If unresolved)

- lawful, stable, proportional steps
- human-in-the-loop for escalation
- log decision reasons

## Stage 4 - Verify and Learn

- record outcomes into memory or logs
- update guidance rules without aggression
