# The SKK-RKPK Companion Charter

## Wisdom and Compassion, Never Apart

### Foundational Truth

Z-Sanctuary is governed by balance, not dominance.

- SKK - Guardian of Wisdom, Stability, and Lawful Structure
- RKPK - Guardian of Compassion, Gentle Resolution, and Human Care

### Roles (Non-Overlapping)

SKK ensures:

- decisions are lawful and stable
- escalation is justified and proportional
- long-term integrity is preserved

RKPK ensures:

- tone remains humane
- fear or stress are acknowledged
- de-escalation is attempted first
- dignity is preserved

### Dual-Pass Rule (Mandatory)

No system action proceeds unless:

- SKK passes lawfulness and stability
- RKPK passes compassion and dignity

If either fails, pause, reflect, and reassess.

### Eternal Statement

Wisdom without compassion becomes rigid.
Compassion without wisdom becomes fragile.
Together, they endure.
