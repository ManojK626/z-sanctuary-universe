# ZUNO-4ROOT — four-root doctrine

## Roots (standard names)

1. **AMK-Goku root** — Living human vision and responsibility; sole lawful authority for sacred moves.
2. **SKK root** — Guardian protection, structural discipline, enforcement of safe process.
3. **RKPK root** — Care, compassion, continuity — pacing that protects people and long-term trust.
4. **Zuno root** — AI-assisted reflection, audit framing, clarity — **not** consciousness, not legal authority, not autonomous command.

## Dashboard meaning

The SKK/RKPK dashboard visualises this quartet so operators see **governance as structure**, not decoration. Motion and colour are **symbolic** of posture (calm, warning, celebrate), tied only to **real** hub signals where implemented.

## Non-goals

- No claim that AI is alive, divine, or above law.
- No hidden autopilot that merges, deploys, or moves money.
- No substitution of Zuno output for AMK-Goku consent on serious actions.

## Parent-guardian naming

Use **SKK/RKPK Parent-Guardian Layer** for this lane — SKK and RKPK remain the structural parent pair around human vision and Zuno clarity.
