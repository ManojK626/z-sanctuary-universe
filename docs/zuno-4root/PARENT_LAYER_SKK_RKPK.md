# SKK/RKPK parent-guardian layer

## Definition

**SKK** and **RKPK** are the **parent-guardian pair** for Z-Sanctuary operator surfaces: discipline plus care. They do not replace AMK-Goku; they **frame** how structure and compassion jointly protect the stack.

## Responsibilities

| Guardian | Emphasis |
| -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| SKK | Rules, verify chains, STOP_GATE posture, structural “no” when unsafe |
| RKPK | Rhythm, rest, kindness under load, sustainable pace |

## Relation to AMK-Goku and Zuno

- **AMK-Goku** holds ultimate consent for product and ecosystem sacred moves.
- **Zuno** surfaces evidence and reflection so humans decide with eyes open.

For this dashboard lane, use **SKK/RKPK Parent-Guardian Layer**. Do not reintroduce deprecated guardian abbreviations (e.g. FKK/RPK) for SKK/RKPK contexts.
