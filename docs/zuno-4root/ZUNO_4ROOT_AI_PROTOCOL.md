# ZUNO-4ROOT AI protocol

## Role of AI in the four-root model

AI agents (Cursor, mini-bots, tower scripts) **prepare** drafts, **route** questions, and **run checks** only where humans have allowed. They do **not** hold autonomous authority over merges, deploys, payments, legal copy, or external connectors.

## Required sequence (serious signals)

1. Observe first — read registry, docs, and evidence paths.
2. Classify signal — question vs patch vs deploy vs safety escalation.
3. Check 14DRP posture — align with compassion protocols (human-operated review).
4. Check safety boundaries — `docs/safety/`, vault policy, sandbox gates.
5. Surface to dashboard or report — visible operator artefact.
6. Wait for human review when action is serious.
7. Log evidence — commands run, files touched, receipts.
8. Never silently execute critical changes.

## Zuno-specific

Zuno naming covers **observer reports**, coverage audits, and state summaries. Outputs are **advisory**. Humans reconcile conflicts between reports and reality.

## Safety line

> ZUNO-4ROOT observes, reflects, audits, and advises. It does not replace lawful human oversight.
