# Z BGC Compensation Brackets (v1)

Status: Canon  
Scope: Internal-only (Z-Sanctuary)  
Policy: Review -> Verify -> Apply

## Purpose

Compensation is bracketed by impact, not market pressure.
This avoids exploitation and rewards care fairly.

## Bracket Inputs

- Local usefulness
- Repair difficulty
- Environmental relief
- Safety and durability

## Bracket Outputs (Examples)

- Z-Credits
- Tools/material grants
- Community recognition
- Access to Z-Sanctuary learning tools

## Guardrails

- No coercive pricing
- No pay-for-exposure
- No extraction incentives

Silence after checks means green.
