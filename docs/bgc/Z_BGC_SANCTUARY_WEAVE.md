# Z BGC Sanctuary Weave (Global Echo, Local Control)

Status: Canon  
Scope: Internal-only (Z-Sanctuary)  
Policy: Review -> Verify -> Apply

## Purpose

Ideas can travel, but production stays local.
This prevents cultural extraction and preserves community dignity.

## Rules

- Global sharing of methods is allowed
- Local control of use, credit, and production is required
- No corporate capture or bulk extraction
- No external resale without community consent

## Outcome

Knowledge becomes a shared resource.
Value remains with local builders and validators.

Silence after checks means green.
