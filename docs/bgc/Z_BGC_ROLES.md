# Z BGC Role Definitions

Status: Canon  
Scope: Internal-only (Z-Sanctuary)  
Policy: Review -> Verify -> Apply

## Z Material Researcher

Role: Finds, documents, and classifies reusable materials.

Responsibilities:

- Log material type, condition, and safety notes
- Capture local context and potential uses
- Avoid extraction or removal without consent

## Z Local Inventor

Role: Repairs, adapts, or repurposes materials into local tools.

Responsibilities:

- Demonstrate durability in local conditions
- Document build or repair steps
- Prioritize repair-first paths

## Z Community Validator

Role: Confirms real-world usefulness and safety.

Responsibilities:

- Test in the environment where it will be used
- Confirm safety and repairability
- Provide feedback without ranking or shame

## Z Mentor (Optional)

Role: Guides newcomers with skill-sharing and care.

Responsibilities:

- Teach without hierarchy
- Reinforce ethical and safety standards
- Respect local knowledge and constraints
