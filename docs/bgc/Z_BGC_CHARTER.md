# Z Bracketing Generous Compensation (BGC) Charter

Status: Canon  
Scope: Internal-only (Z-Sanctuary)  
Policy: Review -> Verify -> Apply

## Purpose

BGC is a dignity-first compensation framework that rewards care, reuse, and local ingenuity
without exploitation, coercion, or extractive pricing.

## Core Principles

- Dignity over scale
- Local control over global extraction
- Repair before invention
- Observation before action
- Gratitude over profit

## What BGC Is

- A structured gratitude engine for community restoration
- A bridge between reuse, environmental care, and local innovation
- A safe, auditable system that values human intelligence and effort

## What BGC Is Not

- Not a speculative marketplace
- Not a predatory gig platform
- Not a competitive race for scarcity
- Not a mechanism for external extraction

## Compensation Brackets (Non-Exploitative)

BGC rewards are bracketed by:

- Local usefulness
- Repair difficulty
- Environmental relief
- Safety and durability in real conditions

Rewards may include:

- Z-Credits
- Tools and materials
- Micro-grants
- Community recognition
- Access to Z-Sanctuary learning tools

## Protection Rules

- No corporate capture or external extraction
- No coercive pricing
- No pay-for-exposure
- No forced disclosure of personal identity

## Enforcement

BGC outputs are observational by default.
Execution is permitted only under Z-Sanctuary governance and Safe Pack allowlist.

Silence after checks means green.
