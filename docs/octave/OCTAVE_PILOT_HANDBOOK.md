# OCTAVE Pilot Handbook (v1.0)

Purpose: Learn, improve, and earn trust—without medical claims.

## What OCTAVE Is

A non-medical comfort and movement support system designed to help people (and pets) feel calmer, move gently, and stay aware of posture and sensitive joints.

It works with or without technology.

## What OCTAVE Is Not

- Not a medical device

- Not a treatment or therapy

- Not a restraint

- Not a replacement for professional care

## How to Use (Simple)

- Wrap lightly. Partial use is fine.

- Move slowly. Stop anytime.

- Escape is instant. Pull the Freedom Line to release.

Optional:

- Use FLOW (gentle movement)

- For kids, GIGGLE (consent-first joy)

- For pets, PAWS (comfort-only)

## Safety First (Always)

- No tightening to “fix” posture

- Avoid sensitive zones (neck, inner joints)

- If uncomfortable, stop and adjust

- OCTAVE never forces the body

## Feedback = Improvement

Your feedback is how OCTAVE improves.

We ask for:

- Comfort & fit notes

- Confusion or friction

- What you’d change

Optional:

- Photos or short clips (date-stamped)

Not allowed:

- Medical claims (cures, diagnoses, treatments)

## How Reviews Work

- Verified reviews are labeled

- Evidence (photos/videos) is optional

- Some feedback triggers public fixes

- You can see changes in “What We Changed”

## Professional Context (Optional)

A licensed professional may acknowledge context (not endorse).
No prescriptions. No diagnoses. Ever.

## Privacy Promise

- No ads

- No data resale

- Optional tech only

- Delete anytime

## If Something Feels Off

- Stop using OCTAVE

- Tell us what happened

- We will respond—and act if needed

Contact: <support@octave-trust.example>

## Final Note

Trust here isn’t claimed. It’s practiced.
Thank you for helping us build responsibly.
