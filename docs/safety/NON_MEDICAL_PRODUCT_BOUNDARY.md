# Non-medical product boundary

**Posture:** wellness, movement, and coaching concepts are **not medical devices**.

## Requirements

- Clear **non-medical / non-diagnostic** disclaimer near any health-adjacent UX.
- Encourage professional care for emergencies and diagnosis.
- Avoid dosing, treatment plans, or condition-specific claims unless a regulated medical path exists (out of hub scope by default).
