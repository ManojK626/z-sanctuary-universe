# MirrorSoul — privacy and sensitivity boundary

**Posture:** doctrine and operator boundary. Not legal advice.

## Scope

MirrorSoul-related surfaces may hold **private reflection, emotional context, and relational labels**. Treat all such data as **high sensitivity** by default.

## Rules (v1)

1. **No silent exfiltration** — data stays in declared stores and paths; no surprise third-party analytics on reflection content.
2. **User-controlled retention** — export/delete flows must remain discoverable where product surfaces exist.
3. **No scoring people** — no hidden “compatibility” or judgment scores surfaced as fact; advisory language only if ever shown.
4. **Minors and vulnerable contexts** — if a surface could reach minors, additional gate is required before launch (human sign-off).

## Evidence hook

Hub packages and routes are listed in `data/z_master_module_registry.json` under `mirrorsoul_hub_slice`. Update this doc when product behavior changes.
