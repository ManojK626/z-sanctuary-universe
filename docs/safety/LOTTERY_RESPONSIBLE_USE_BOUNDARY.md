# Lottery / trust portal — responsible use boundary

**Posture:** public-trust and gambling-adjacent visibility. Not legal advice.

## Principles

1. **No guaranteed outcomes** — copy and UI must not imply wins, edge, or certainty.
2. **Jurisdiction** — operators confirm local law before any real-money linkage.
3. **Transparency** — odds, limits, and data use are stated plainly where the product speaks to users.
4. **Trust portal** — `Amk_Goku Worldwide Loterry/ui/public_trust/trust_portal.js` is a **claims and ethics surface**, not a payout engine.
