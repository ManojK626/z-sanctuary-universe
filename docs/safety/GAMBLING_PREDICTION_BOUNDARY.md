# Gambling and prediction boundary

**Posture:** prediction and gaming-adjacent modules are **advisory and non-authoritative**.

## Copy rules

- Avoid **guaranteed win**, **sure thing**, or **beat the house** language.
- Frame outputs as **observations, simulations, or journals** — not financial advice.

## Product tie-ins

- Roulette and calculator workspaces in the registry are **safety_hold** until compliance copy is wired.
- Any automation that places bets or moves funds is **out of scope** without a separate human gate.

## Prediction

- **gambling_prediction_voice** and similar concepts stay **doctrine or stub** until this boundary is reflected in UX and tests.
