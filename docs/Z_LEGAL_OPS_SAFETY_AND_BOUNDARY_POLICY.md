# Z-LEGAL-OPS-1 safety and boundary policy

## Phase posture

**Z-LEGAL-OPS-1** is concept governance only:

- docs
- JSON registries
- policy flags
- read-only validator reports
- dashboard indicator metadata

No legal runtime services are opened.

## Locked law

```text
Lawyer dashboard ≠ lawyer endorsement.
Legal circle ≠ runtime authority.
AI evidence summary ≠ legal advice.
Named lawyer persona ≠ consent.
Contract required before real advisor role.
Client data requires privacy/security charter.
GREEN ≠ launch.
BLUE requires AMK/legal/human review.
RED blocks movement.
AMK-Goku owns sacred moves.
```

## Identity and public-claim rules

- No named real lawyer profile without written consent.
- No endorsement statement without signed agreement.
- No claim that AI is a solicitor/lawyer.
- No "specialist superiority" marketing claims from this phase.

## Data boundary

- No client data intake.
- No evidence upload runtime.
- No privileged data storage.
- No SAR/DSAR automation.
- Users control sharing decisions.

## Runtime boundary

- No auth/account creation.
- No provider calls.
- No filing/email/payment execution.
- No deployment lane opening from legal ops validator.
