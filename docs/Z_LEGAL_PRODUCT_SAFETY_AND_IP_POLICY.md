# Z-LEGAL-PRODUCT-OPS-1 — Product safety and IP policy

## Policy posture

This phase keeps product-legal operations in **read-only governance mode**.

## Core product-law boundary

```text
Product map ≠ product launch.
Prototype evidence ≠ safety certification.
Invention log ≠ patent filing.
IP candidate ≠ registered right.
Manufacturer list ≠ contract.
```

## Safety and claim controls

- No medical or health claim publication without professional review.
- No safety certification claim without evidence.
- No connected-device data flow before data protection review.
- No child-safety-sensitive lane widening without explicit gate.

## IP controls

- Candidate lists are not legal registrations.
- Patent/trademark/design filings require professional legal route and filing proof.
- Public claim wording must avoid implying filing success before evidence exists.

## Manufacturing controls

- No supplier connector runtime in Phase 1.
- No manufacturing commitments from dashboard state.
- No payment activation or purchase-order lane.

## Lawyer identity controls

- No lawyer endorsement without signed agreement.
- No named real lawyer in public UI without written consent.
- AI summaries support review but do not provide legal advice.
