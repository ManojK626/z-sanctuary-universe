# Z-MAINTENANCE RITUAL

Goal: 15 minutes, once a week. Calm, not perfection.

---

## 15-Minute Flow

1. 2 min - Scan alerts and decide: BLOCKER, MAINTENANCE, or NOISE.
2. 5 min - Fix 1-2 doc lint items (MD040, MD036 only).
3. 5 min - Fix 1 small code lint item (no-unused-vars, prefer-const).
4. 3 min - Note 1 improvement idea and stop.

---

## Rules

- Stop at 15 minutes even if more is left.
- If you feel tired, skip to next week.
- Avoid large refactors during maintenance.
- Use the Codex to decide what is safe to ignore.
