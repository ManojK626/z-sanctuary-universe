## Universal agent questions

Every AI worker should answer before it suggests movement:

1. Where am I?
2. Which repo/project am I in?
3. What may I read?
4. What may I change?
5. What is forbidden?
6. Which signal is active?
7. Does AMK need to decide?
8. What is the smallest safe next action?
9. What evidence proves it?
10. What rollback exists?
