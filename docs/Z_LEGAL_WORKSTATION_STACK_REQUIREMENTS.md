# Z-LEGAL-WORKSTATION-STACK-1 — Lawyer workstation stack requirements

## Purpose

Define a complete, lawyer-grade workstation requirement map for full-cycle legal operations while preserving Phase 1 governance posture:

- read-only requirements and policy
- report-only validation
- no runtime legal authority

This phase is the architecture spine for later gated implementation slices.

## Tool stack domains

1. Matter and case workspace
2. Evidence intake and chain of custody
3. Media review suite (video/audio)
4. Document intelligence and legal drafting support
5. Review and approval gates
6. Communications flow hub
7. Product/IP/safety/compliance lanes
8. Security/privacy/retention controls
9. Simulation mode (safe training)
10. Audit export and receipts

## Required capabilities (Phase 1 as requirements only)

- Multi-format evidence coverage: docs, images, audio, video.
- Immutable evidence IDs and hash-based integrity checks.
- Timeline and issue queue map for counsel review.
- Traceable AI output with source references and confidence.
- Human-owned legal approval before client-facing advice.
- Structured communication templates for legal handoff clarity.
- Exportable evidence packets and decision receipts.

## AI role posture

AI roles may exist as governance definitions:

- Evidence Librarian AI
- Transcript AI
- Chronology AI
- Contract Compare AI
- Risk Spotter AI
- Communications Drafter AI

These roles are assistants for indexing, summarization, and drafting suggestions only.

## Locked law

- Workstation power != runtime legal authority.
- AI workflow help != legal advice.
- Media processing != permission to share.
- Simulation mode != live client operations.
- Dashboard visibility != endorsement.
- GREEN != launch.
- BLUE requires AMK/legal/human review.
- RED blocks movement.
- AMK-Goku owns sacred moves.
