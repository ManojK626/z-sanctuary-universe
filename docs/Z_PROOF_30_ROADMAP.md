# Z-PROOF 30 Roadmap

## Objective

Deploy a 30-day trust-hardening layer without disrupting Z-Sanctuary development velocity.

## Week 1 (Foundation)

- Enable proof card generation (`z_proof_mesh_card.mjs`).
- Enforce proof gate check (`z_proof_gate_check.mjs`).
- Confirm protection audit + privacy freshness in daily runs.

## Week 2 (Evidence Quality)

- Increase module registry coverage toward 30%+.
- Reduce open P1 backlog with priority sync.
- Verify signed artifacts are present in trust-pack verification paths.

## Week 3 (Governance Tightening)

- Review request-only policy effectiveness.
- Validate token-based deep access flow for approved requests.
- Confirm research intake remains allowlist-only and manual-adoption.

## Week 4 (Readiness Rehearsal)

- Run full proof sweep daily for 7 consecutive days.
- Keep proof card status at `green` for at least 5/7 days.
- Prepare external-facing summary from proof metrics only (simple facts).

## Non-Negotiables

- No automatic model/module replacement from external research.
- No deep disclosure without signed, time-limited approval.
- No release escalation while proof gate is red/hold.
