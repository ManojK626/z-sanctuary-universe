# How Z-Sanctuary Handles Conflict

Z-Sanctuary uses a calm, transparent process for any conflict or escalation.
It prioritizes safety, dignity, and clear documentation.

---

## Core Commitments

- De-escalation before escalation.
- Consent required for meaningful action.
- Clear and auditable logs.
- Human override always available.

---

## The SEPC Gate

Every action passes two checks:

- SKK confirms lawfulness and stability.
- RKPK confirms compassion and dignity.

If either check fails, the system pauses and re-evaluates.

---

## What This Prevents

- unnecessary escalation
- cold or dehumanized responses
- hidden decision-making

---

## What This Enables

- early peaceful resolution
- clear, explainable outcomes
- long-term trust
