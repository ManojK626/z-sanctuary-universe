# Z-UCCR — Guardian Panel Requirements

**Purpose:** Define **guardian-class** canvases as **symbolic plus operational oversight** surfaces. They amplify compassion, STOP gates, and audit awareness—not personality impersonation.

**Doctrine:** Spiritual/mythic names (**SKK, RKPK, Hashirai, Vegeta, Ghost, Alien, Bee Vision**) are **cultural and operational symbols** anchored in Sanctuary lore. Panels must quote **roles and posture**, not simulate private individuals or give medical/legal/financial advice.

---

## 1. What guardian panels may show

- Posture summaries (observe / caution / STOP).
- Risk band (conceptual—not casino-style risk scores).
- Warnings grounded in verifier output or sanctioned policy text.
- **STOP_GATE** or **pause** motifs when release control or enforcer says hold.
- Audit note pointers (link to receipt, not raw sensitive logs).
- Compassion and safety reminders tied to DRP posture (see [Z-ULTRA-INSTINCTS-AND-FORMULAS.md](../Z-ULTRA-INSTINCTS-AND-FORMULAS.md)).

---

## 2. What guardian panels must not do

- Impersonate real people (voice, chat, or “I am X” without explicit authorized fiction labeling).
- Make medical diagnosis, legal determinations, or financial instructions.
- Override AMK-Goku / Overseer / release-gate authority.
- Auto-execute irreversible actions (delete vault, purge NAS, stripe charge, governance flip).
- Shame operators or induce panic visuals (flash, scream audio) unless user explicitly opts into drill mode—with reduced-motion safe path.

---

## 3. Symbolic guardian lanes (planned)

Document how each Guardian **representation** behaves in UI drafts. Voices are labels; visuals are motifs.

### SKK (Sanctuary Keeper Kernel)

- **Tone:** Steward of calm boot and honest status.
- **Surface:** Lattice or soft grid halo; amber when attention needed.

### RKPK (Root Keeper Pulse Key)

- **Tone:** Rhythm of hubs and workspaces; pulses at heartbeat speed only if motion allowed.
- **Surface:** Gentle pulse rings; disables when reduced motion.

### Hashirai (Path Watcher)

- **Tone:** Cross-project drift and misalignment watch—points to EAII/registry docs.

### Vegeta (Discipline / Standards)

- **Tone:** High bar for verify truth; never humiliates—shows gaps as fixable receipts.

### Ghost (Phantom Audit)

- **Tone:** Highlights hidden debt (stale todos, orphaned modules)—spectral metaphors subtle.

### Alien (Extrinsic View)

- **Tone:** Outside-in readability—what would a careful stranger see?

### Bee Vision (Collective Care)

- **Tone:** Colony health without extracting individual nectar—aggregated pollinator motifs.

Note: Exact lore copy may live in guardians manifest docs; canvases cite those links—do not invent conflicting canon here.
