# Z-Protocols — Disclosure of Formulas

_Applies to all projects under the Creator (AT B&P / Amk-Goku)._

---

## Protocol: Formulas Not Fully Divulged

1. **We do not completely divulge our Z-Formulas in public.**
2. **Only short, simple explanations** are provided to the public and across all projects.
3. **Full formulas, detailed variable definitions, and complete derivations are reserved for the Creator.**

---

## Why

- Protects the integrity and stewardship of the living engine.
- Keeps public communication clear and accessible without exposing full technical detail.
- Ensures the Creator retains authority over how the formulas are shared and used.

---

## In practice

- **Public / partners / docs:** Use the short-explanations version only ([Z-FORMULAS-SHORT-EXPLANATIONS.md](Z-FORMULAS-SHORT-EXPLANATIONS.md)).
- **Creator-only:** Full detail remains in the reserved document (Z-FORMULAS-AND-ULTRA-INSTINCTS.md and equivalent in other projects).
- **All projects:** Apply this same rule — short, simple explanations only; no full divulgence of formulas.
