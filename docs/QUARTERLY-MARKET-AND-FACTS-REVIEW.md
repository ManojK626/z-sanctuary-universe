# Quarterly market and facts review

**Role:** Human ritual for refreshing comparables, cost assumptions, and regulatory posture. **Z-SUSBV** references this cadence; it does not replace human judgment.

## Cadence

- **Quarterly** (calendar or fiscal — pick one and record in your first review note).
- Capture: comparable services, public list prices (with URLs), cost-to-serve deltas, trust/compliance changes.

## Outputs (human-owned)

- Review notes in your vault or ticket system (not auto-written by Z-SUSBV Phase 1).
- Updates to `data/z_susbv_benchmark_registry.json` and `data/z_susbv_service_price_validation.json` only after human edit.
