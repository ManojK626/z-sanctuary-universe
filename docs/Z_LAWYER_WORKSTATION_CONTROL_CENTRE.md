# Z-LEGAL-OPS-1 — Z-Lawyer Workstation Control Centre

Formal ID: **Z-LEGAL-OPS-1** (layer: `z_lawyer_workstation`).

## Purpose

Describe a future **private legal review surface** for contracted professionals to inspect evidence and governance posture. In Phase 1, this is a **concept/dashboard metadata layer only**.

## Panel map

- Ecosystem map
- Risk gate panel
- Evidence receipts
- Data protection panel
- AI safety panel
- XBUS external connectors
- Contract status
- Lawyer review queue
- Audit export concept

## Identity and endorsement boundary

```text
Real lawyer name in UI requires written consent.
No endorsement claims without signed agreement.
No AI impersonation of real lawyers.
```

Use placeholders only in concept docs:

- `Z-Legal Review Mentor A`
- `Z-Legal Review Mentor B`
- `Z-Tom Legal Mentor AI` (synthetic internal persona)
- `Z-Julianne Legal Mentor AI` (synthetic internal persona)

## Live authority boundary

This workstation does **not** grant runtime legal authority. It is a review cockpit for evidence and policy posture.

- No auth/account system in this phase.
- No public legal service claim.
- No legal advice delivery runtime.
- No client upload lanes.
- Persona cards are synthetic/internal and do not imply real-lawyer consent or endorsement.

## Review outputs (allowed in concept phase)

- alignment summary
- risk note
- missing policy metadata note
- decision packet draft for AMK/legal review
