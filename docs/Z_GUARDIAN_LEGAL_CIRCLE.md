# Z-LEGAL-OPS-1 — Z-Guardian Legal Circle

Formal ID: **Z-LEGAL-OPS-1** (layer: `z_guardian_legal_circle`).

## Purpose

Define the boundary for a future real-world legal advisor circle. This layer is governance-only in Phase 1 and is not a runtime legal authority mechanism.

## Core rule

```text
Z-Guardian Legal Circle is real-world professional governance
only under written agreement.
```

## Contract and consent gates

- Written agreement required before named advisor role.
- Written consent required before any public profile naming.
- Scope of review must be explicit and human-approved.
- No implied endorsement from dashboard presence.

## Forbidden in Phase 1

- named lawyer public profile without consent
- legal advice claim from AI
- client case management runtime
- privileged client data storage
- automated legal communications
- treating synthetic mentor personas as real-lawyer profiles

## Dual Knowledge Spine

This phase adopts a **Dual Knowledge Spine** concept:

1. **Z-Sanctuary Knowledge Spine** — modules, reports, receipts, gates, policies.
2. **Legal/Compliance Knowledge Spine** — legal notes, contract status, review outcomes.

Bridge rule:

```text
AI may compare and summarize.
Lawyers decide legal advice.
Humans decide sharing and action.
```

Expansion note: when product/IP/manufacturing lanes are reviewed, the same contract and consent gates apply; guardian legal circle remains outside runtime authority. See [Z_LEGAL_PRODUCT_SAFETY_AND_IP_POLICY.md](Z_LEGAL_PRODUCT_SAFETY_AND_IP_POLICY.md).

Persona note: labels like `Z-Tom Legal Mentor AI` and `Z-Julianne Legal Mentor AI` are internal synthetic advisor personas for governance prototypes only — not real-lawyer endorsement or consent.
