# Z-VH100 — Vegeta & Harisha 100 Security Core

**Purpose:** A **defensive security posture engine** — the **warrior-shield mindset** behind resilience and warning systems — **not** an attack surface, **not** surveillance, **not** a replacement for human moral gate.

## Two pillars (mythology → doctrine)

### Vegeta pillar — defense and challenge

- **Defense posture:** protect the Sanctuary’s systems, backups, lineage, and service integrity.
- **Challenge logic:** pressure-test assumptions (“Is this drift harmless?”, “Is this duplicate safe?”, “Does this automation violate ZAG?”) — **in policy, reviews, and charters** — not by hitting external targets.
- **No nonsense:** refuse silent overwrite, hidden autonomy, or undeclared scope creep.

### Harisha / Hashirai 100 — mentor–guardian council

- **Mentor–guardian discipline:** proportionality, care, and **100-point protection mindset** — completeness of checks before widening blast radius.
- **Council, not crown:** guardians **advise and gate**; **AMK / human** still owns escalation on sacred, commercial, and privacy paths.
- **Honor:** protect the community **without** becoming the threat (no deanonymization, no VPN games, no stalking metadata).

## Phase VH100-1 scope

- **Docs + metadata only** (`data/z_vh100_security_posture.json`).
- **No** scripts, scanners, offensive tooling, network surveillance, VPN deanonymization, auto-blocking, auto-restore, auto-delete, provider calls, APIs, deployment, billing, or live bridges.

## Escalation law

- **14 DRP / 14 DOP** and Hierarchy Chief govern **all** escalation beyond light advisory posture.
- VH100 **classifies risk** and **recommends human-gated responses** — it does **not** adjudicate law or replace DRP review.
