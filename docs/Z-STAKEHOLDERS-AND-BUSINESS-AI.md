# Z-Sanctuary: Stakeholders, Partners & Users — ID, Dashboards, Payment & Business AI

**Purpose:** Future stakeholders, partners, and users of Z-Sanctuary technology are put **first** with high ID verification, dedicated dashboards, worldwide payment with 14 DRP Protocols, and a complete organisation layer powered by Z-Business AI, Z-HR AI, Z-Financials Observer & Regulator AI, and Z-Business Joker AI. From Inner Kindness — processing workloads and relieving stress at the same time.

---

## 1. High ID verification protocols (by category)

- **Categories:** Define distinct verification tiers (e.g. User, Partner, Stakeholder, Operator, Regulator) so each role has the right level of trust and access.
- **Protocols:** Document and implement high ID verification (e.g. KYC-style where applicable, device/session binding, optional MFA) per category. Z-Sanctuary keeps security and consent at the centre; no role gets more access than verified for.
- **Different categories, different dashboards:** Each category has **its own dashboard** (or dashboard view) so partners, HR, finance, and operators see only what they need. Governance and SEPC apply to all.

---

## 2. Dedicated dashboards per category

- **User dashboard:** Already in place (Z-SSWS dashboard, panels, Blueprint, Legal & Help, etc.). Extend with optional on/off/auto/Read modes for sections.
- **Partner / Stakeholder dashboard:** Focus on commitments, impact, Pool of Generosity (see Z-Jobless AI Reminder), and high-level metrics. Links to 14 DRP and terms.
- **Operator dashboard:** For Z-Business & Accountant AI operators — workflows, ledgers, approvals, and Z-Financials Observer alerts.
- **HR dashboard:** For Z-HR AI (Human Resources Secretary) — multi-domain HR, policies, wellbeing, and pathways. Feeds into Z-Stories Of Z Day and Z-FB-Reflection where relevant.
- **Regulator / Observer dashboard:** For Z-Financials Observer & Regulator AI — read-only or gated views on compliance, thresholds, and give-back % (aligned with 14 DRP and Z-Jobless AI Reminder).

All dashboards use the same **Canvas/Blueprint pattern** and optional **on/off/auto/Read** mini-options where suitable.

---

## 3. Worldwide payment gateway & 14 DRP Protocols for Business

- **Payment gateway:** Worldwide coverage (existing matrix: PayPal, Stripe, Revolut, SEPA, Wise, UPI, etc.) with clear fees, currency, and region rules. See Huge file and Register for wallet/payment specs.
- **14 DRP Protocols for Business:** Document the 14 protocols (Disclosure, Responsibility, Protection — or your exact DRP expansion) that govern how Z-Sanctuary and partners handle money, give-backs, and accountability. These feed into Terms & Conditions and into Z-Financials Observer & Regulator AI so every flow is auditable and fair.
- **Integration:** Z-Business & Accountant AI and Z-Financials Observer use the same gateway and DRP rules; Z-HR AI can reference payroll or benefits flows where applicable.

---

## 4. Z-Business & Accountant AI (Operators)

- **Role:** Operators’ AI for day-to-day business and accounting workflows — invoicing, ledgers, approvals, and reporting within 14 DRP and governance.
- **Integration:** Works with the payment gateway, Z-Financials Observer (for compliance), and Z-HR AI (for payroll/benefits). Has its own operator dashboard.
- **Ethos:** From Inner Kindness — efficiency without cruelty; automation that frees humans for higher-value work.

---

## 5. Z-HR AI — Multi-domains (Human Resources Secretary)

- **Role:** Multi-domain Human Resources Secretary — policies, onboarding, wellbeing, leave, performance pathways, and cross-link to Z-Stories Of Z Day and Z-FB-Reflection for feedback and pathway identifiers.
- **Integration:** Partner/stakeholder dashboards, operator dashboard, and Regulator dashboard where HR metrics or fairness are relevant. Uses same ID verification and consent layers.
- **Ethos:** People first; HR as enabler, not gatekeeper. Align with Z-Jobless AI Reminder so no one is left behind.

---

## 6. Z-Financials Observer & Regulator AI

- **Role:** Observes and regulates financial flows — compliance with 14 DRP, thresholds, give-back %, and Pool of Generosity. Read-only or gated regulator dashboard; alerts to operators and governance when rules are at risk.
- **Integration:** Payment gateway, Z-Business & Accountant AI, Z-Jobless AI Reminder (give-backs, Aimanity pool), and Terms & Conditions. No conflict of interest: Observer does not execute payments.
- **Ethos:** Transparency and fairness; protect the vulnerable and the planet.

---

## 7. Z-Business Joker AI (when all jobs are done)

- **Role:** For fun and stress relief when core jobs are complete. Light tone, humour, and “relieving stress” so the organisation stays human and joyful. From Inner Kindness — genius is not only productivity but also care and laughter.
- **Integration:** Optional; can surface in operator or user dashboard as a “Joker” mode or short break. Does not override governance or financial rules.
- **Ethos:** Complete organisation = processing workloads **and** relieving stress. Amk-Goku: genius from inner kindness.
