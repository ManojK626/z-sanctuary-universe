# Civilizational foresight — safety law (Z-FUTURE-1)

**Applies to:** Z-PPPFA, Z-MSOAI, Z-FUMCR, and any future foresight runtime **only after** explicit charter and human gates.

## Core law (use verbatim in high-risk contexts)

```text
Past pattern ≠ future certainty.
Scenario ≠ prophecy.
Signal ≠ instruction.
Roadmap ≠ permission.
AI overseer ≠ human authority.
GREEN ≠ launch.
BLUE requires AMK / human review.
RED blocks movement.
```

## Doctrine commitments

1. **Civilizational foresight is not prophecy.** We explore **branches** and **uncertainty**, not ordained outcomes.
2. **Past-to-present-to-future work is scenario discipline.** Methods borrow from **foresight** and **risk literacy**; they do not certify the future.
3. **Z-MSOAI overseers are review roles.** They structure questions; they do **not** hold legal, moral, or operational **authority** over humans.
4. **Z-FUMCR roadmaps are migration maps.** They describe possible transitions; they are **not** user instructions, deploy scripts, or entitlement changes.
5. **14 DRP / 14 DOP** govern claims about people, safety, and operations. No foresight doc bypasses DRP or hub build rules.
6. **Human review** is required before any **high-impact conclusion** is treated as org policy, public claim, or launch rationale.
7. **RED / YELLOW / GREEN / BLUE** (and related hub signals) are **advisory gates** — they do not replace release control, counsel, or AMK sacred-move discipline.
8. **No public claim** shall state that Z-Sanctuary **predicts** civilization outcomes with certainty.
