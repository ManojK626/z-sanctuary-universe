# Z-LEGAL-PRODUCT-OPS-1 — Legal canvas world-map workstation

## Canvasi meaning (safe)

In this phase, **Canvasi** means:

- visual proof canvas
- legal review board
- product family tree
- risk heatmap
- evidence timeline

It does **not** mean surveillance, tracking people, scraping, or automated legal decision engines.

## Visual zones

- AI services
- physical products
- IP candidates
- manufacturing pathways
- contracts
- data protection
- risk gates
- evidence receipts
- world/market concepts

## Boundary

```text
Canvas map ≠ live tracking.
Visual board ≠ legal authority.
AI summary ≠ legal advice.
```

## Phase 1 constraints

- No live map tracking.
- No personal data processing from map surfaces.
- No external map APIs.
- No provider calls.
- No launch or manufacturing execution hooks.
