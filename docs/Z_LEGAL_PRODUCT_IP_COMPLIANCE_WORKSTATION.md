# Z-LEGAL-PRODUCT-OPS-1 — Lawyer Product and IP Compliance Workstation

Formal ID: **Z-LEGAL-PRODUCT-OPS-1**.

## Purpose

Define a future contracted-advisor workstation where legal professionals can review the full Z-Sanctuary product reality across software, physical products, IP, safety, manufacturing pathways, compliance, and evidence.

Phase 1 is **governance concept only**:

- docs
- registries
- policy
- read-only validator
- indicator metadata

No launch, filing, manufacturing, payment, or deployment authority is granted.

## Six workstation worlds

1. AI/software world
2. Physical product world
3. IP/creation proof world
4. Manufacturing/supplier world
5. Compliance/safety world
6. Canvas/world-map proof world

## Required review lens

Every product lane must stay in this order:

```text
idea → evidence → review → prototype → safety → IP → contract → launch
```

Never skip directly from concept to public claim.

## Status model

- **Invented / documented** — concept and receipts exist.
- **Review candidate** — legal/IP/safety/manufacturing review required.
- **Commercially cleared** — only after evidence-backed human legal and safety clearance.
