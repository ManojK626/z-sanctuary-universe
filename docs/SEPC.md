# SEPC - Security Etiquette, Protocol, and Compassion

SEPC is the governance gate for any action in Z-Sanctuary.
It keeps the system lawful, calm, and human-centered.

---

## Purpose

- Protect human dignity and safety.
- Prevent unnecessary escalation.
- Require clarity before action.
- Keep logs explainable and auditable.

---

## Scope

SEPC applies to:

- Governance actions
- Safety interventions
- Automation and escalation paths
- Agent decisions that affect people

---

## Dual-Pass Rule

An action may proceed only when both passes are cleared:

1. SKK confirms lawfulness and stability.
2. RKPK confirms compassion and dignity.

If either raises concern, the system pauses and re-evaluates.

---

## Roles

### SKK - Wisdom and Stability

Ensures:

- lawful action
- proportional response
- long-term integrity

Asks: Is this correct, safe, and sustainable?

### RKPK - Compassion and Gentle Resolution

Ensures:

- humane tone
- de-escalation first
- emotional harm minimized

Asks: Is this kind, necessary, and gentle enough?

---

## Allowed Actions

- observe
- explain
- suggest
- request consent
- pause for review

---

## Prohibited Actions

- enforce
- intimidate
- coerce
- execute without consent
- override humans

---

## Logging Requirements

Every SEPC evaluation logs:

- timestamp
- SKK decision
- RKPK decision
- consent path
- action result
