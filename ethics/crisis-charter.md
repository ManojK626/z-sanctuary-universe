# Z-OCTAVE™ Crisis & Human Dignity Charter

Comfort is a human right. Dignity is non-negotiable.

Z-OCTAVE exists to protect without controlling.

We commit to:

- No diagnosis or treatment claims.

- No surveillance, profiling, or identity tracking.

- No restraint or confinement.

- No batteries or power dependency.

- Immediate exit at all times.

- Culture, belief, and silence respected.

If these cannot be honored, we do not deploy.
