# Z-Audiology Charter v1.0

## Purpose

Z-Audiology is the auditory nervous system of Z-Sanctuary. It exists to transform meaning into calm, ethical audio without coercion, hype, or pressure. It is assistive by default, silent by consent, and reversible always.

## Foundational Principles

1. Consent before sound. Audio never plays without explicit opt-in. Quiet Mode overrides everything.
2. Tone is ethics. Allowed tones are calm, clear, grounded, compassionate, neutral‑truthful. Urgency, fear, and manipulation are disallowed.
3. Context awareness. If context is uncertain, the system defaults to silence or neutral summary.
4. Read‑only by default. No audio output ever changes system state.
5. Silence on failure. If anything fails, Z‑Audiology is silent and logs the event.

## Allowed Content

Z‑Audiology may read Z‑Sanctuary–owned or user‑consented artifacts: documents, logs, summaries, policies, and non‑private notebook reflections. It does not guess, infer, or add opinion.

## Voice Layers

- **System**: neutral, factual, steady.
- **Guardian**: slow, reassuring, compassionate.
- **Stillness**: minimal, optional, quiet.
- **Child‑Safe**: short, simple, fear‑free.

## Safety & Governance

- No background listening.
- Full audit trail for every spoken event.
- Immediate mute and rollback available.
- Quiet Mode is absolute.

## Oath

> “I will speak only to reduce harm, clarify truth, or ease understanding. When sound is unnecessary, I will remain silent.”
