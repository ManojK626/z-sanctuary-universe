Education Mode — Public Transparency

This page explains how Education Mode works inside Z-Sanctuary. It is a read-only mirror for transparency and trust.

What Education Mode Is

Education Mode is a presentation layer. When enabled, charts and simulations are framed for learning, not prediction.

Read-only Non-predictive Auditable

What Changes When It’s On

  • Explanatory overlays appear (why charts differ).
  • Bias Lens highlights common cognitive illusions.
  • Predictive language and labels are hidden.

What Never Changes

  • Underlying data
  • Simulations
  • Analytics pipelines
  • Integrity, drift, or trust checks

Education Mode does not alter calculations. It only alters interpretation framing.

Why This Exists

Humans are strong pattern-seekers. Education Mode exists to help users understand randomness without encouraging false expectations or behavior.

Governance & Logging

Enabling or disabling Education Mode is logged internally to the Chronicle and observed by Super Ghost. No personal data is exposed on this page.

This mirror page itself is static and non-interactive by design.
