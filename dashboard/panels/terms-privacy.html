Z-Sanctuary Member Terms & Privacy

Internal member manual for Z-Sanctuary Universe. This is a high-level overview and does not replace any jurisdiction-specific legal agreement you may sign in the future.

1. Who these terms are for

These terms apply to members using the Z-Sanctuary Universe dashboards, rituals, AI assistants, and automation tools in a personal or business capacity.

Z-Sanctuary is designed as a safety-first, harmony-focused environment. All automation is assistive. You remain in control of final decisions.

2. Account, identity & sign-in

  • Each member logs in with a verified identity (workspace account, local account, or configured identity provider).
  • Sign-in events may be logged with time, device, and environment to support security monitoring and audit.
  • If unusual activity is detected, Security Sentinel can slow or pause automation until a human review is complete.

3. Fair use of services

  • Do not use Z-Sanctuary to harm others, commit fraud, or evade law.
  • Do not resell or misrepresent AI outputs as “guaranteed outcomes.” They are guidance, not promises.
  • Critical financial, legal, or irreversible actions should always include a human review step.

4. Privacy & data handling

Z-Sanctuary treats your information as private by default. Data is used to run the systems, improve reliability, and personalize guidance.

  • Audit logs store minimal context needed to understand “what happened” for safety and debugging.
  • Where external AI services are used, only scoped, relevant snippets are sent, never full private archives without explicit consent.
  • Where supported by law and implementation, you may request export or deletion of personal data.

5. AI assistants & responsibility

Z-Secretary AI and other assistants (Harisha, Super Ghost, etc.) provide explanations, recommendations, and drafts based on your configuration and inputs.

  • You remain responsible for reviewing outputs before acting on them.
  • For sensitive topics (health, finance, legal, safety), always consult a qualified human professional.
  • Conversations may be logged to the Governance and Consent Center for transparency and safety review.

6. Feedback, questions & disputes

If something feels unclear, unfair, or broken, you can:

  • Open the Legal & Consent Center from the dashboard.
  • Ask Z-Secretary AI for a human-readable explanation.
  • Review specific events via the “Why Did This Happen?” audit explainer.

Z-Sanctuary aims to resolve issues through dialogue, transparency, and repair wherever possible.
