What it is

A planned universe map and orientation layer for the full Z-Sanctuary hub: modules, guardians, reports, safety posture, and future panels—so operators see the forest before touching tools.

What it is not

  • Not a live control panel with execution power.
  • Not an automation hub or agent swarm launcher.
  • Not a source of data authority (receipts and verifiers remain canonical).
  • Not a path to Sovereign Vault contents.

Cousin relationship

XL2 Storm Canvas (outside this repo) = product workstation for that pilot app. Z-UCCR = whole-universe doctrine realm for Z-Sanctuary only. Cousins, not merged—no XL2 coupling from this panel.

Guardian panels (summary)

Future guardian surfaces are symbolic and oversight: posture, STOP signals, audit pointers, compassion reminders. They do not impersonate real people, give medical/legal/financial advice, or override human authority. Detail: docs/universal-canvas/GUARDIAN_PANEL_REQUIREMENTS.md (open from repo).

Accessibility promise

  • Mute / no audio by default for any future canvas media.
  • Calm mode and plain focus mode for overwhelm and density.
  • Reduced motion: no required animation to read state.
  • No flashing defaults; readable contrast goals.
