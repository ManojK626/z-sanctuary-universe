Respectful Integration Model

Z-Sanctuary provides direct links to trusted radio services, respecting their platform boundaries and user experience. No external embeds. No CORS conflicts. Pure discovery links that honor each service's independence.

Why links, not embeds? External radio platforms restrict embedding for good reason. Direct links are faster, more reliable, and respect their business models. Users get instant access to 10,000+ stations across all services.

  • Shoutcast: Peer-to-peer network · Independent operators · Highest quality diversity
  • TuneIn: Curated catalog · Professional + amateur · Podcast integration
  • Radio Garden: Community-driven · Interactive discovery · Global perspective

All links open in new tabs. All services respect user privacy and content rights. Z-Sanctuary collects zero usage data from these links.
