# z_music_curator_bot (assist-only)

## Role

- Suggest playlists and track order from **tagged catalog** data.
- Respect mode and guardian filters.
- **Never** auto-play.
- **Never** write entitlements, billing, or cross-project memory.

## Outputs

Curator output must always include the [explainability contract](../explainability_contract.md) block for the operator UI.
